About this page

We, at Blueberryπ are determined to aid everyone in the need of hour, when the times are dark and lives are at risk, we are trying our best to reach everyone in need. This initiative is aimed at being a hassle free common platform between the people with resources that are ready to lend a hand and the people in need, saving lives and keeping you updated about the current situation.

Services we offer

  • Plasma Donors
  • Blood Donors
  • Oxygen cylinder suppliers
  • Remdesivir availability
  • Find Covid cases-statistics
  • Find Hospital beds
  • Find Covid vaccination centres PAN India
  • Food Resources for Covid affected
